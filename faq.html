Frequently Asked Questions

  • What payment methods do you accept? ▾
    We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), mobile payments, and bank transfers.

    Unopened items may be returned within 14 days for a refund. Opened or used products are eligible for exchange only if faulty. Contact us at scentedperfumes1@gmail.com to begin a return or exchange.
  • Are your perfumes authentic? ▾
    Yes — all products sold are genuine and sourced from approved suppliers. We perform quality checks on every shipment before sale.
  • Can I buy wholesale or place bulk orders? ▾
    Yes. For wholesale inquiries or bulk pricing, email our team at scentedperfumes1@gmail.com with your requirements and we'll respond with pricing and minimum order details.
  • How do I track my order? ▾
    After dispatch you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use that number on the courier's website to monitor delivery status.
  • I applied for a job — when will I hear back? ▾

  • How can I contact customer support? ▾

  • Do you ship internationally? ▾
    Yes — we ship to many countries. International shipping costs and delivery times depend on destination and will be calculated at checkout.

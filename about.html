About Scented

Scented is a premium fragrance and lifestyle brand rooted in clarity, elegance, and founder-grade execution. We craft gender-neutral scents and self-care essentials that elevate everyday rituals—without compromise. Every product is designed and developed by our founder, Constance, with obsessive attention to detail across formulation, packaging, and brand identity.

From our signature fragrances to our expanding line of lotions, diffusers, and cosmetics, Scented is built for those who value simplicity, sophistication, and sensory impact. We believe luxury should be universal, and every label, font, and drop reflects that belief.

Scented is more than a scent—it’s a standard.
